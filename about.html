Why do I use Github Pages?


I've always wanted my own website, and found this to be the perfect learning opportunity to learn HTML and CSS. For any non-github users or people less familiar with technology, this is a place for me to provide more detail on my projects. Not only that, but most people wanting to download one of my projects, most likely don't want to read paragraphs of text in the 'readme' file. This is where I can explain what projects I'm working on, why I made them, and go into greater detail into how they work and my thought process behind them. Combine that with hosting a website for free using Github Pages, led to the perfect opportunity to make my own site. Thanks for reading!
